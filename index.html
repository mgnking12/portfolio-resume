About Me

“Any sufficiently confident technology is indistinguishable from magic"
― Arthur C. Clarke

The first chapter of my coding life began with a good ole fashioned Myspace layout. I loved learning about the magic my computer would carry out to create the awesome colors on my screen. I loved it so much that I would make a new layout for my personal page every few weeks. I then decided to monetize this skill and charged a dollar for public use of any layouts I had created previously. I was quite the young entrepreneur at the age of 13!

Ten years later and I am at it again. At the end of 2014, I decided to dive back into my first love and discovered Javascript. This new found knowledge allowed me to become a Jr. Front End Developer. As I grow professionally, so does my curiosity. Even though I have a better understanding of the magic of coding, I'm not done unraveling it. Currently, I am exploring all of it as a student with the University of Texas Full Stack Coding Bootcamp. This opportunity has allowed me to discover new names and methods to the magic that was so fascinating to me years ago.
